Chamber Site Plan

Overview

Purpose

The goal of this website is to provide a link where economically disadvantaged would have the opportunities
to earn something for a living .

Knowing the stigmatizations ex-convicts faces in jobs in the society; the purposes of the site is the bridge that gap,
and provide them with jobs the can be doing to earn a living while finding their way to reintegrate into the society.

Audience

People who are economically disadvantaged, ex-convicts that are finding it difficult to be accepted into the labor market

Scenarios

Are you economically and finnacially disadvantaged?

Are you troubled that you cannot provide for yourself and love once?

Are you troubled on how to make a living in a world that stigmatize ex-convicts?
